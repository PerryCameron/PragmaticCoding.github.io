I was lucky enough to notice right at the beginning of my career that lots of IT departments struggle to succeed. It's hard, and I made a conscious decision to pay attention and learn from the way that the people around me succeeded, and even more attention to how they failed. For the past 35 years I've spent a lot of time thinking about how we think about building systems.
